Kingslayer

Project Description

Kingslayer was an academic project I embarked on in the class IAT 265: Multimedia Programming for Art and Design.

Kingslayer is a challenging bullet hell where the avatar is pit against several bosses in the form of levels, where the player has to dodge against the bosses’ onslaught of attacks wielding only a sword and a pistol.

Through this project, I aimed to explore how to design fast-paced gameplay by taking inspiration from the platform bullet hell genre.

Features

Kingslayer features 3 incremental bosses with their own individual attack patterns, which increases in difficulty and complexity as the boss’s health diminishes, creating high-action gameplay that keeps players on their toes.

Kingslayer also includes a shop that allows players to upgrade their abilities after every boss, and difficulty options that allow players to play at the level they’re comfortable with.
